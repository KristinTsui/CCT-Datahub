About Us

The Cancer Cell Therapy Data Hub is a collaborative initiative to accelerate cancer cell therapy research and innovation. We leverage public data, advanced computational methods, and machine learning tools to analyze clinical and preclinical datasets, nominate promising engineered cell designs, and foster a collaborative ecosystem for data-driven discoveries.

Mission & Vision

Mission:

To accelerate cancer cell therapy research and innovation by leveraging public data, advanced computational methods, and machine learning tools to analyze clinical and preclinical datasets, nominate promising engineered cell designs, and foster a collaborative ecosystem for data-driven discoveries.

Vision:

To establish a world-leading hub that integrates all available public datasets, cutting-edge computational methods, and generative AI models to revolutionize cancer cell therapy research, driving the development of more effective and personalized treatments for patients with cancer.
